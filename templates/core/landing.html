About Centennial Christian Seminary

Centennial Christian Seminary is a co-ed boarding missionary school located in Kongowe–Mkuranga, Coast Region, Tanzania. Founded in 2011, CCS is registered under number 4615 and operates under the Tanzania Presbyterian Church (TPC).

Our vision is to deliver high quality education in a nurturing environment, foster curiosity, teach responsible citizenship, and equip students with optimal Christian education based on John 8:31-32 (NIV).
